Facilitation Philosophy

Mastery requires frequent practice.
Encourage students to guide and teach each other.
Increased collaboration leads to better results for all.
Challenge tasks on assignments keep advanced students engaged.
Rewarding students for asking questions encourages more student research.
Keep focused tasks short.
Expect more from students than they think they are possible of.
Humour and surprise helps maintain attention to tasks.
Humour and surprise helps students remember lessons.
Occasional competition helps build class cohesion.
A more cohesive group learns faster and deeper.
Always provide more material than students need to complete assignments.
